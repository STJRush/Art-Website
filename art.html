Art in St.Joseph's

Art is one of the many choice subjects in St. Joseph's.
In the first year of school, students will get to experience all choice subjects and eventually choose two to work towards for Junior Cert

"The syllabus, for first, second and third year students in post primary education, aims to develop the expressive, communicative and functional modes of art, craft and design in the individual within the art class with drawing as the central activity." - Curriculum Online

Portfolio

The Art portfolio for Junior Cert requires:

1. Three A2 sheets filled with paintings related to theme.
2. Two A2 sheets filled with artist info related to theme.
3. One A2 painting related to first prep sheet.
4. One clay or wire frame model relating to your second prep sheet.
5. One etching or lino print related to your third prep sheet.
