Portfolio

The Art portfolio for Junior Cert requires:

1. Three A2 sheets filled with paintings related to theme.
2. Two A2 sheets filled with artist info related to theme.
3. One A2 painting related to first prep sheet.
4. One clay or wire frame model relating to your second prep sheet.
5. One etching or lino print related to your third prep sheet.
